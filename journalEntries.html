20XX-05-30

I am on a solo mission in orbit around the Earth in the FX-09 Space Shuttle. It is powered by the Dell Optiplex 760, one of the most technologically advanced computers from this year. It supports an Intel Core Duo E8400 processor with 6MB of cache memory, with an included 2GB of DDR2 ram and an added extra 2GB of ram. This system unit has Intel GMA X4500 integrated graphics and a 640GB, high-tech hard drive.

I have discovered an issue in my system unit. Thus, I need to stop over a planet to see what went wrong.

- Safwan Khan
